Our Project Portfolio

At Airbel, we believe good ideas can come from anywhere. We consider a wide range of innovations, have a high appetite for risk and experimentation, and become more selective as ideas advance through the R&D process. We use stage gates to determine how to invest and resource projects as they move through key development milestones. We prize "fast learning" and aren't afraid to pivot, discontinue or graduate projects as appropriate. Learn more about the IRC Innovation Fund and our project criterion.
